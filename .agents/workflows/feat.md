description: Create a feature with highest quality, implement related dependencies, rate it, and iterate to improve it.
---
When the user executes `/feat` or starts their request with `/feat`:
1. Implement the requested feature with the highest standard of craftsmanship, premium UI/UX, and robust engineering.
2. Proactively analyze the feature requirements and identify any other related features, modules, configurations, assets, files, or dependencies needed to support it. Implement all of them.
3. Once the initial implementation is completed, perform a critical self-review:
   - Rate the feature (quality, responsiveness, UX/UI, robustness) on a scale of 1-10.
   - List detailed, constructive reasons for the rating.
4. Based on the self-review, identify areas of improvement and immediately execute a refinement round to upgrade the implementation, polishing the codebase and design to perfection.
